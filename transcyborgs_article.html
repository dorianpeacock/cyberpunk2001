Either way, it doesn’t matter what you or I think because medically speaking, it’s the other way around. All trans people are cyborgs. Any “elective” surgeries or procedures that don’t fall into the realm of traditional plastic surgery have to be done by a techno-biologist, rather than a normal medical professional. That’s HRT, voice training, top and bottom surgeries, going through the same system as you do for your laser eyes and robo limbs.

“B-but,” you may feel the need to say, “those aren’t enhancements! They’re just changes! And let’s face facts here, unless you know someone who knows someone, cyborg maintenance is a rich person’s game. People are dying because cyborg maintenance is a rich man’s hobby, a rich man’s playground, a rich man’s science lab.
